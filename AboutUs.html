ABOUT US

The Story of Svalegaarden in Hasseris

In 1952, the beautiful grocery farm was rebuilt on the corner of Thorsens Allé and Svalegårdsvej, and the farm was then renamed Svalegaarden. The idyllic farm is still at the same address and has set the framework for many events and experiences over the years.

In June 2016, Svalegaarden got new tenants with new spirits and bright hopes for the future - Mette and Stefan from the former Restaurant Mest. Mette and Stefan come with new energy and fresh eyes on the beautiful renovated grocery farm, which has so far served as a corporate business.

Svalegaarden Most today

Mette and Stefan have a vision to take the best from Restaurant Mest and carry it on in the farm's beautiful setting in the heart of Aalborg. The thoughts behind Svalegaarden Most gastronomy meet rural idyll, where the focus is on creating memorable experiences.

The first years with Svalegaarden Most Svalegaarden Most is wonderful to work with. The old buildings are felt and give us a natural humility and desire to pass on the good experiences to our guests. We have found ourselves well adapted in Hasseri's work with many different angles in gastronomy. Svalegaarden Most includes everything from private parties, meeting facilities, lectures to food out of the house.
